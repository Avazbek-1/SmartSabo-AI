## 🇺🇿 Loyiha haqida (O'zbek tilida)

**"Smart Sabo"** — bu eshitish va gapirishida nuqsoni bor vatandoshlarimizning jamiyatga integratsiyalashuviga yordam beruvchi innovatsion loyiha. Bizning maqsadimiz — o'zbek imo-ishora tilini real vaqt rejimida matn va ovozga aylantiruvchi raqamli ko'prik yaratish.

### 🚀 Hozirgi holat va Hackathon maqsadi
Ushbu repozitoriyda bizning **Hand Tracking (Qo'lni aniqlash)** bo'yicha dastlabki ishlanmalarimiz joylangan. Ilgari biz bu texnologiyani notebook tizimini qo'l harakati bilan boshqarish uchun ishlatganmiz. 

**Farg'ona AI Hackathon (Mart 26-28)** davomida biz ushbu bazaviy texnologiyani tubdan transformatsiya qilmoqdamiz:
1.  **Dataset yig'ish:** O'zbek imo-ishora tilining (O'zIIL) bazaviy lug'atini shakllantirish.
2.  **AI Model:** Oddiy harakatlardan so'zlarni va jumlalarni taniy oladigan **LSTM (Long Short-Term Memory)** neyron tarmog'ini qurish.
3.  **Voice Output:** Tarjimani o'zbekcha nutqqa (TTS) aylantirish.

## 🇺🇸 About the Project (English)

**"Smart Sabo"** is an AI-driven initiative designed to empower individuals with hearing and speech impairments in Uzbekistan. Our mission is to build a digital bridge that translates Uzbek Sign Language (O'zIIL) into real-time text and audible speech.

### 🛠 Current Progress & Hackathon Goals
This repository contains our initial **Hand Tracking** framework. Previously, we utilized this technology for gesture-based laptop control.

During the **Fergana AI Hackathon (March 26-28)**, we are evolving this foundation into a full-scale translator:
1.  **Data Collection:** Building a comprehensive dataset for essential Uzbek Sign Language gestures.
2.  **Deep Learning:** Implementing an **LSTM-based model** to recognize sequences of movements as meaningful words.
3.  **Accessibility:** Integrating Text-to-Speech (TTS) to make communication seamless in public spaces like banks and hospitals.
